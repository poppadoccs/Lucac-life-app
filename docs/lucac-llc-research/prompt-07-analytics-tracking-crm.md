## Research subject

Research how Lucac should track whether the site actually works — without
overcomplicating it for a one-person shop.

Include:
- Plausible vs GA4 (privacy, simplicity, cost, what each is good for)
- Microsoft Clarity pros / cons (heatmaps, session replay, privacy)
- Google Tag Manager — yes or no for a site this size
- events to track:
  - form submit
  - form error
  - chatbot open
  - chatbot message sent
  - chatbot lead intent
  - CTA clicks
  - click-to-call
  - email click
  - pricing CTA
  - care plan CTA
  - audit CTA
- UTM capture (carrying source through to the form)
- source_page field (which page the lead came from)
- referrer capture
- simple CRM options: Airtable, Notion, Folk, Attio, HubSpot free, etc.
- how to avoid overcomplicating it (the minimum that delivers signal)

## Required output
